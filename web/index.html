Send a bridged asset back to unlock the original on Ethereum. Create a redemption address bound to your Ethereum address, then send the bridged asset to it from any Sequentia wallet. The vault releases the locked funds only after the return transaction's Bitcoin anchor is buried deep enough that a Bitcoin reorg cannot undo it, not simply once the transfer confirms on Sequentia. On Sequentia, Bitcoin anchoring is the supreme consensus rule, so a confirmed Sequentia transaction can still be reversed until its Bitcoin anchor is final.
